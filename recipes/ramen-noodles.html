Ramen Noodles

Description

Ramen is a great dish for when you're sick, or its winter time, or you just want something savory and delicious.

This recipe has the ramen noodles you need, the seasonings and ingredients we recommend as well.

It has a soft boiled egg, Nori better known as seaweed and some other suggestions to spice up your dish.

Ingredients list

  • Ramen noodles of your choice
  • Mushrooms
  • Soft-boiled egg
  • seaweed
  • green onions
  • radish

Steps

  1. Fill up a pot with water and let come to a boil
  2. Once boiling add your uncooked egg to it for 7 minutes
  3. After egg is done put it in an ice bath
  4. Throw some onions in a sauce pan until their golden brown
  5. Throw your noodles in a pot of boiling water for 3 minutes or until your preferred doneness
  6. Add all your ingredients in a bowl with the broth from the ramen
  7. garnish with scallions
