Ramen Recipe

Description

Perfect for a winter day or when sick!

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Seasoning packet
  • Canned corn
  • Trader Joes pork belly

Steps for cooking

  1. Boil water and add noodles cook for 3 minutes.
  2. Strain corn.
  3. Add pork belly to air fryer at 400 for 5 minutes.
  4. Once noodles complete strain half of water and add seasoning packet.
  5. Add corn and pork belly
  6. Enjoy!
