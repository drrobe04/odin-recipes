Smack yo momma fried chicken recipe

Description

Fried chicken is a delicious meal anyone can enjoy

Its great for a summer cookout

Its perfect for families

Ingredients list

  • Chicken drumstrcks
  • Flour
  • eggs
  • oil

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and beat until even
  2. put flour on a plate
  3. put your chicken in the egg
  4. put your chicken in the flour
  5. put chicken in your oil
