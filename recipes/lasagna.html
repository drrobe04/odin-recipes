Better than grandmas lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a great dish for feeding a group

It is also very customizable so if you have any picky eaters or allergies you can switch it up as you like

Ingredients list

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
