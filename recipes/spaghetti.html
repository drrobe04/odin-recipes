Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is a classic recipe for spaghetti with meatsauce.

A quick and easy family dinner for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Pasta sauce with meat
  • Mushrooms
  • Salt

Steps for cooking

  1. Boil water and add healthy amount of salt.
  2. Add noodles and cook till al dente.
  3. Strain noodles and add pasta sauce.
  4. Let sit on low heat and stir to combine noodles and sauce.
  5. Sautee mushrooms.
  6. Add into pot with spaghetti and sauce and stir till combined.
  7. Plate and enjoy!
